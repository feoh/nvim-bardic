*nvim-bardic.txt*        Neovim support for Bardic interactive fiction

===============================================================================
CONTENTS                                                     *nvim-bardic*

nvim-bardic provides Neovim support for Bardic `.bard` files.

Commands:                                                *nvim-bardic-commands*

:BardicCompile        Compile the current Bardic story. (planned)
:BardicLint           Lint the current Bardic story. (planned)
:BardicGraph          Show the story graph. (planned)
:BardicGraphRefresh   Refresh the visible graph. (planned)
:BardicGraphExport    Export the story graph. (planned)
:BardicPreview        Preview the passage under the cursor. (planned)

Configuration:                                           *nvim-bardic-config*
>
  require('bardic').setup({
    bardic_cmd = 'bardic',
    timeout_ms = 10000,
  })
<
